Small & Mighty

Every day throughout the campaign we’ll introduce you to three different nonprofits in the Pikes Peak region you should get to know.

DayBreak - An Adult Day Program


DayBreak provides the at-home, 24/7 caregiver the opportunity to regroup, recharge, and refresh while their loved one engages in meaningful activity and social interaction in our "home away from home" setting - preventing a long-term care placement (for their loved one) before its time.

Colorado Springs Pioneers Museum


CSPM is brimming with activity, excitement and energy.

Harley's Hope Foundation


Harley's Hope Foundation believes in the value of the human-animal bond and helps keep pets and service animals in their homes with people who love them, but who are struggling with life's challenges.

What is the Give! Campaign?

Give! is an effective, efficient and fun annual philanthropic platform that provides valuable media exposure, volunteers, board development and much-needed financial support to excellent, innovative and often little-known Pikes Peak region nonprofits.

How do rewards work?

While giving is and should be its own reward, local businesses wanting to support Give! and our local nonprofits provide a selection of cool gifts at no cost to Give!, as a special and unique THANK YOU to you for your donation. The more money you donate, the more rewards you get. Young donors who give $10 or more are eligible for their own unique rewards. Link to rewards »

Can I donate by mail or over the phone?

Yes. Donors who do not wish to donate online may mail donations to: Give!, 235 S. Nevada Ave, Colorado Springs, CO 80903. Please make your check payable to INDYGIVE and be sure to specify what organization you are donating to and include your address, phone, and email address. If donating to multiple organizations please combine onto one check and include a list of the individual amounts you’d like applied to each organization.
