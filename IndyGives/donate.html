Donate

To make a donation, enter in the amount in the box next to the nonprofit of your choice. You can make donations to multiple nonprofits. You can also donate on each individual nonprofit’s page on this website. Once you are finished, click on the Checkout button to complete your donation.

If you wish to make a gift In Honor or Memory of someone, you will have the opportunity to do so when you check out. Please note you can only recognize one person per transaction. You will need to check out additional times if you wish to recognize multiple people.

Each donation must be $10.00 or more. Please update your donations that are less than $10.00 and continue checkout.
